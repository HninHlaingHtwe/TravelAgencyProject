    Yangon-Mawlamyaing-Ngapali

    Tour Package (Yangon-Mawlamyaing-Ngapali)

    Myanmar has many things to do and to see, so if your time is limited, this 5-day itinerary will cover the main highlights of Yangon, Old Capital Mandalay, Ancient City Bagan and Lake Inle. By using domestic flights, you’ll get to spend more time in each region.

    • Half day Yangon city highlights tour
    • Golden Rock
    • Exploring colorful Hpa-An
    • Private coach transportation between Yangon, Hpa-An and Mawlamyaing

    Your Jounery Day By Day

    Day 1 : Arrival Yangon


    Arrive at Yangon International Airport in the morning. Meet with your guide and then transfer to your hotel for check-in (if early check-in is available).


    Day 2 : Yangon – Bago – Kyaikhtiyo


    Enjoy breakfast at hotel. Early morning, drive out of Yangon to the ancient capital of Bago. Visit Shwemawdaw Pagoda, the highest pagoda in Myanmar and Bago’s golden landmark.


    Day 3 : Kyaiktiyo – Hpa-An – Mawlamyaing-setse


    After breakfast, walk down the mountain and return by truck to your car/minibus, or alternatively walk all the way down to the base camp at Kinpun (leaving very early morning if you choose to do this).


    Day 5 : Mawlamyaing – Yangon
