    Yangon-Bagan-Inle lake

    Tour Package (Yangon-Bagan-Inle lake)

    Myanmar has many things to do and to see, so if your time is limited, this 5-day itinerary will cover the main highlights of Yangon, Old Capital Mandalay, Ancient City Bagan and Lake Inle. By using domestic flights, you’ll get to spend more time in each region.

    • Yangon city highlights tour
    • Walk among thousands of ancient temples in Bangan
    • Stay at water cottage in Inle Lake with Outback tour
    • Domestic flight between Yangon, Bagan and Lake Inle
    Enquiry Now

    Your Jounery Day By Day

    Day 1 : Arrival Yangon


    Arrive at Yangon International Airport in the morning. Meet with your guide and then transfer to your hotel for check-in (if early check-in is available).


    Day 2 : Yangon - Bagan


    This morning, transfer to the airport to leave for Bagan. From the airport, start your full day Bagan sightseeing tour with a visit to Nyaung U Local Market.


    Day 3 : Bagan – Heho – Inle Lake


    Breakfast at hotel. This morning, transfer to Nyaung Oo Airport for your domestic flight from Bagan to Heho.


    Day 4 : Inle Lake – Heho – Yangon


    Pick up from your hotel in Inle and transfer to Heho Airport for your domestic flight to Yangon. Arrive at Yangon Airport and drive to visit Kaba Aye Pagoda (World Peace Pagoda).


    Day 5 : Yangon


    Breakfast at hotel. This morning, transfer to Karaweik Place.


    Day 6 : Sule


    Breakfast at hotel. This morning, transfer to Sule pagoda.
