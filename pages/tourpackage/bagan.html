    Yangon-Mandalay-Bagan-Inle

    Tour Package (Yangon-Mandalay-Bagan-Inle)

    Myanmar has many things to do and to see, so if your time is limited, this 6-day itinerary will cover the main highlights of Yangon, Old Capital Mandaly, Ancient City Bagan and Lake Inle. By using domestic flights, you’ll get to spend more time in each region.

    • Half day Yangon city highlights tour
    • Exploring former capital Mandalay
    • Stay at water cottage in Inle Lake with Outback tour
    • Domestic flight between Yangon, Mandalay and Lake Inle
    Enquiry Now

    Your Jounery Day By Day

    Day 1 : Arrival Yangon


    Arrive at Yangon International Airport in the morning. Meet with your guide and then transfer to your hotel for check-in (if early check-in is available).


    Day 2 : Yangon – Mandalay


    After breakfast at hotel, transfer to Yangon airport for your domestic flight from Yangon to Mandalay. From the airport, drive to Amarapura and visit Mandalay Kyone.


    Day 3 : Mandalay – Bagan


    After breakfast at hotel, transfer to Mandalay airport for domestic flight from Mandalay to Bagan. From the airport, start your full day Bagan sightseeing tour with a visit to Nyaung U Local Market.


    Day 4 : Bagan – Inle


    Depart hotel after breakfast for the domestic flight from Bagan to Heho. Transfer from Heho airport to Nyaungshwe on the edge of Inle Lake and board your own local boat to take you to your hotel.


    Day 5 : Inle – Yangon – Departure


    Breakfast at hotel. This morning, transfer to Heho Airport, domestic flight from Heho to Yangon. Arrive at Yangon International airport and then leisure till your international flight.
