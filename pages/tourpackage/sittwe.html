    Yangon-Mrauk U-Sittwe

    Tour Package (Yangon-Mrauk U-Sittwe)

    Myanmar has many things to do and to see, so if your time is limited, this 5-day itinerary will cover the main highlights of Yangon, Old Capital Mandalay, Ancient City Bagan and Lake Inle. By using domestic flights, you’ll get to spend more time in each region.

    • Half day Yangon city highlights tour
    • Exploring Ancient Arakan Kingdom
    • Stay at water cottage in Inle Lake with Outback tour
    • Domestic flight between Yangon, Bagan and Lake Inle
    Enquiry Now

    Your Jounery Day By Day

    Day 1 : Arrival Yangon


    Arrive at Yangon International Airport in the morning. Meet with your guide and then transfer to your hotel for check-in (if early check-in is available).


    Day 2 : Yangon – Sittwe


    Pick up from your hotel, in Yangon and transfer to Yangon airport for domestic flight to Sittwe. Arrive at Sittwe, transfer to your hotel. Lunch at a local restaurant or hotel.


    Day 3 : Sittwe – Mrauk U


    Enjoy breakfast at hotel. This morning, transfer to Sittwe Jetty where you board your private boat to Mrauk U. When we reach to Mrauk-Oo, transfer to local Restaurant for late lunch. After lunch, check into your hotel.


    Day 4: Mrauk U – Chin Tattoo Village – Mrauk U


    Enjoy breakfast at hotel. This morning, visit Mrauk U’s Main Market and Koe Thaung Temple. Continue to Lay Myo river bank. And then proceed by boat to Chin Tattoo area.


    Day 5 : Sandway – Yangon – Departure


    Breakfast at hotel. This morning, transfer to Thandwe Airport for your flight back to Yangon. Arrive at Yangon Airport and leisure till your international flight.
